The Language of Red Roses

Red roses have captured minds and hearts for centuries. Universally recognized as the symbol of profound love, passion, and romance, a bouquet of luxurious red roses communicates what words often cannot. The deep ruby hue signifies respect, admiration, and devotion, making it the quintessential gift for significant others and grand romantic occasions.

How to Make Them Last

  • Trim the stems: Cut 1-2 inches off the stems at a 45-degree angle under running water.
  • Use clean water: Fill a clean vase with lukewarm water and add the provided floral food packet.
  • Remove foliage: Ensure no leaves are submerged below the waterline to prevent bacterial growth.
  • Keep cool: Display your bouquet in a cool location, away from direct sunlight, heating vents, and ripening fruit.
  • Change water: Replace the water entirely every 2-3 days and re-trim the stems slightly for optimal hydration.
